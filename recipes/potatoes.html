Mashed Potatoes

These are the best mashed potatoes. You can pair them with your favorite comfort food, or, honestly, eat them as a meal by themselves. They come out buttery smooth and packed with that classic mashed potatoes flavor.

Disclaimer:

This is not my recipe. I am using One Girl. One Kitchen's perfect-mashed-potatoes-for-two recipe linked at the bottom of this page.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Put potatoes, peeled and diced, in a pot of cool water and add 1.5tsp of salt.
  2. Boil water and let potatoes cook for 25 minutes.
  3. Drain potatoes and add back to a pot, letting the potatoes dry for about a minute.
  4. Add butter, remaining salt, and heavy cream. Stir until combined.
  5. Mash potatoes to a smooth consistency.
  6. Serve with extra butter because you know you want to and put whatever spices you want in it. Enjoy!
original recipe from One Girl. One Kitchen.
