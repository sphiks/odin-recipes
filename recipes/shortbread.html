Shortbread cookies!

This shortbread recipe is to die for. The cookies come out with a perfect balance of butter and sweetness. And the best part? It only need 4 ingredients you probably already have in your pantry!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 cups all-purpose flower

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit, and gather your ingredients.
  2. Beat softened butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in vanilla, and add flour gradually while mixing.
  4. Form cookies and place on an ungreased cookie sheet, spaced about an inch apart. Then bake in the preheated oven until the edges of the cookies are brown.
  5. Remove from the oven and let sit for 3-4 minutes. Then, enjoy your amazing cookies!
